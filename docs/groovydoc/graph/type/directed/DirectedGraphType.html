Package: graph.type.directed

[Groovy] Class DirectedGraphType

      • graph.type.directed.DirectedGraphType

      • This type changes the behavior of a Graph to that of a directed graph.

        The following modifications are made to the graph:

        members

        edges
        All edges are converted to DirectedEdges and all future edges will be DirectedEdges. Any two vertices may be connected by one or two DirectedEdges. Two DirectedEdges connecting any two vertices cannot have the same direction. Direction is determined by the values of DirectedEdge.getFrom and DirectedEdge.getTo. The values of one and two cannot be the same in any two DirectedEdges.
